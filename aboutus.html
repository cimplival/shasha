ABOUT US

Our Mission

  • 1 Provide 1 Million students access to education by 2020.
  • 2 Become a leading online education platform for students in Sub-saharan Africa.
  • 3 Offer innovative technology which is responsive to the needs of a 21th century learner.

Who We Are

Founded in 2014, Co-founder Farai Munjoma who was still in High school at the time faced challenges preparing for examinations as his teacher had left his school. Realising the internet's potential not only to harness information but also to connect learners and educators all over, him and his Co-founder Simbarashe Murondoti and their team, embarked on a journey to provide students access to online education and ensure that every student achieves their fullest potential and unleashes the champion in them. Through this affordable and innovative technology they aim to become the number one leading online education provider in Sub-Saharan Africa by creating an interactive nexus between students and sophisticated learning material and educators.
